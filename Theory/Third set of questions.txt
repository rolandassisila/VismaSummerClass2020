5. What are CSS sprites? How can you manipulate them?
It is a image that is made out of several different images. Basically it is used to optimize the load up of certain elements where images from sprites are used. They can be manipulated bu adjusting width and height of background by positioning certain part of an image. 
6. What is the difference between adaptive and responsive design?
Responsive resign adapts to the size of the screen being used. Adaptive design is uses static layouts for a specific device.
7. What does 'Mobile first' mean?
It means that the site is being build for mobile first and the design adapted to different screen sizes.
8. What is media queries? What are the common media queries breakpoint values?
@media is used to apply specific fragments of styling to a specific screen sizes.
